Lasagna

Description

This homemade lasagna features layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. Baked until golden and bubbly, it's a comforting Italian-inspired dish that's perfect for family dinners, gatherings, or meal prep. Rich, hearty, and full of flavor, this classic asagna is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 700g (24 oz) tomato sauce
  • 425g (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until the beef is browned.
  4. Add the tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  9. Top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20–25 minutes, until golden and bubbly.
  12. Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

A creamy and comforting pasta dish made with tender chicken, fettuccine noodles, and a rich Parmesan Alfredo sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh parsley (optional)
  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions.
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet and cook the chicken until golden and cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  5. Melt butter in the same skillet and sauté the garlic.
  6. Add the heavy cream and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  7. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until smooth.
  8. Add the cooked pasta and chicken to the sauce.
  9. Toss until evenly coated.
  10. Garnish with parsley and serve.
